iRipple (pronounced as Eye-Ripple) Overview

Company Summary

iRipple  is a Philippine retail technology company that provides an integrated retail operating platform for retailers. The platform combines point-of-sale (POS), retail management, inventory control, customer loyalty, business intelligence, and AI-powered retail analytics into a single ecosystem. iRipple has over 25 years of experience serving the retail industry and supports more than 400 retail brands, over 4,000 stores, and more than 1 million daily retail transactions.  

The company’s mission is:

Lead retailers to greatness through technology.

Its vision is:

Retailers can scale up and speed up using iRipple technology.  

⸻

Core Value Proposition

iRipple helps retailers improve daily store operations by addressing common retail challenges such as:

iRipple Platform

The iRipple platform consists of interconnected retail solutions that operate on shared retail data.

1. POS
2. Inventory & Purchasing

5. Concessionaire Management

Products

Barter POS

Barter POS is iRipple’s enterprise point-of-sale system.

Purpose

Provides fast and reliable checkout operations for retail stores.

Key Capabilities

* Barcode, SKU, and product lookup
* Multiple payment methods
* Cash, card, e-wallet, and store credit support
* Split payment processing
* Promotion and discount engine
* Returns and exchanges management
* Offline transaction processing
* End-of-day reporting
* Cash reconciliation
* Automatic synchronization after connectivity restoration

Business Benefits

* Faster checkout throughput
* Reduced cashier errors
* Improved customer experience
* Continuous operations during internet outages

Typical use cases include supermarkets, department stores, hardware stores, specialty retail, and multi-branch retail operations.  

⸻

Barter RMS (Retail Management System)

Barter RMS is a centralized retail management platform for multi-store operations.

Purpose

Provides head-office control over inventory, pricing, purchasing, promotions, and operational governance.

Key Capabilities

* Centralized inventory management
* Real-time stock monitoring
* Reorder point management
* Price management
* Supplier management
* Purchase order management
* Store-to-store transfers
* Promotion planning
* User role and access management

Business Benefits

* Improved inventory accuracy
* Better purchasing control
* Consistent pricing across stores
* Standardized operations across branches

Barter RMS is intended for retailers operating multiple stores that require centralized governance and operational control.

Purpose

Detects suspicious transaction activity and potential fraud.

* Discount abuse
* Cashier anomalies
* Transaction exceptions
